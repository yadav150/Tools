Privacy Policy

Skill2Job values the privacy of its users and is committed to ensuring that any personal information provided while using our website and online tools is protected. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide the services you request. Any data collected is solely intended to enhance user experience and deliver timely and relevant updates about government jobs, admit cards, results, tutorials, schemes, and online tools offered on our platform.

Our online tools, such as PDF converters, image resizers, and image combiners, are designed to process information locally on the user's device whenever possible. This minimizes the need for uploading sensitive files to our servers and helps maintain user privacy. Non-identifiable usage data may be collected to improve the performance, functionality, and usability of the website and its tools. This allows us to provide a more seamless experience for job seekers and students accessing our services.

Users are encouraged to remain cautious when sharing personal information online. Skill2Job strongly advises verifying any job-related updates, results, or admit cards from official government portals before taking any action. By using our website and tools, you consent to the privacy practices outlined in this policy and acknowledge that the responsibility for verifying details lies solely with you. We ensure that data security measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access and misuse.

In addition to protecting your personal data, Skill2Job is committed to maintaining transparency in our practices. We regularly review and update our privacy policies to comply with applicable regulations and improve the safety and reliability of our services. Our goal is to provide students and job aspirants with a secure and trustworthy environment, allowing them to focus on their preparation without concerns about data security or privacy.
